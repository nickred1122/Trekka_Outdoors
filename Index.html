Trekka Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 26, 2025

1. Introduction

Trekka ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Health Data

With your explicit permission, Trekka accesses health data from Apple HealthKit, including:

  • Heart rate and heart rate variability (HRV)
  • Sleep analysis data
  • Activity data (steps, calories, workouts)
  • VO2 Max, respiration rate, and oxygen saturation

Location Data

Trekka uses your device's GPS to track workouts and create routes. Location data is only collected when you actively record a workout.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to:

  • Provide personalized fitness recommendations and coaching
  • Track your workouts and fitness progress
  • Display health metrics and recovery scores
  • Generate training plans and insights

4. Data Storage and Security

All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit, store, or process your health data on external servers. Your privacy is our priority.

5. Third-Party Services

Trekka integrates with third-party services (Garmin Connect, Strava, Oura, WHOOP) only with your explicit consent. Authentication is handled through secure OAuth 2.0 protocols.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke health data permissions at any time through iOS Settings
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app
  • Disconnect third-party integrations from within the app

7. Children's Privacy

Trekka is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected with a new "Last Updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@trekka.app
